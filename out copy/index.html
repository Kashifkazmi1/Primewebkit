Features

Everything you need to launch a support agent

From training to analytics, PrimeWebKit covers the full lifecycle of a production chatbot.

Crawl your website

Point PrimeWebKit at your site and it indexes every page as chatbot knowledge — no copy-pasting.

Documents & Q&A

Upload PDFs, DOCX, or CSVs, or write Q&A pairs directly for the answers that matter most.

Streaming answers

Responses stream token-by-token, powered by Google Gemini with retrieval-augmented context.

Lead capture

Collect name, email, and phone mid-conversation and route new leads straight to your CRM.

Analytics

Track top questions, response times, satisfaction ratings, and lead conversion in one view.

Webhooks & API

React to events like new leads or completed chats in real time, or drive everything from your API.

Team roles & permissions

Invite teammates with scoped roles — owner, manager, member — across shared chatbots.

White-label

Remove PrimeWebKit branding and use your own domain and colors on paid plans.
